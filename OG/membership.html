The Breakaways is a small, social club and encourages its members to participate in all aspects of running a successful and enjoyable organization.

The current Club Charter limits the total number of Full Members (family group) to 50.

Annual Club membership fees are currently $65 - which includes affiliation fees to FWDSA.

Joining Fee is currently $25.00.

There are two types of Membership: Full and Social.

Qualifications for membership are:

  • Full Membership - attend two (2) Club meetings and two (2) overnight designated Club trips in the Club year.
  • Social Membership - attend two (2) Club meetings in the Club year. (Social Members cannot hold executive positions).

On becoming a member you will be able to take part in the training that is offered by the Club's volunteer Advisors, Trainers and Assessors.

Members can purchase Club T-Shirts in various sizes and styles. Also caps available.
