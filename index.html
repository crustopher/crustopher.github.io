Welcome to the site of The Breakaways 4WD Club of SA.

The Breakaways are a not-for-profit organization formed for like-minded Four Wheel Drivers in the north-east area of Adelaide.

The Club conducts group 4WD activities and enables members to be educated and trained in correct, responsible, and environmentally friendly, vehicle operating techniques.

We encourage visitors and prospective members to participate in our Club trips and activities and gain invaluable experiences. Take a browse and see the various trips that have occurred previously and those that are coming up.

If you require further information on any aspect of the Club do not hesitate to contact any of the committee members who would be pleased to help you.
